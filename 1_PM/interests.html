Interests

In addition to my interests in CS and technology, I enjoy studying philosophy, playing sports, and speaking French and Italian. Technically Italian was my first language because much of my family is from Bologna. I always looked forward to visiting my family in Italy and playing soccer in the parks and beaches. My love for sports extended into high school where I played on the varsity soccer team and picked up squash, and crew. In school, I enjoyed studying the philosophical concepts of virtue and the nature of the human self in the Western tradition. With a friend, I was motivated to create a supplement to my school's philosophy course that focussed on Eastern philosophical traditions such as Hinduism, Confucianism, and Mohism. Outside the classroom, I work as a Case Manager at the Y2Y homeless shelter in Harvard Square. I am a member of the Harvard open Data project where I collect, clean, and analyze data to gain deeper insights about past events and predict future ones. I also joined the Harvard Computer Society to pursue my interests in the tech field.
